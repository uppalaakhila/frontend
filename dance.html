DANCE

BHARATNATYAM

Bharatanatyam as a repertoire is very geometric, forceful, systematic and linear. It requires great precision in understanding the formation of shapes, lines, and formations. To amply demonstrate these concepts in an online Bharatanatyam dance class for a beginner as well as an advanced student, needs a great amount of physical space as well as sound concepts in the technique.

KUCHIPUDI

Kuchipudi, a pre-eminent Indian classical dance form counted among ten leading classical dance forms of India, is a dance-drama performance art that originated in a village of Krishna district of Andhra Pradesh, India.Kuchipudi is evolved as a religious art rooting back to the age-old Hindu Sanskrit text ‘Natya Shastra’ and connects traditionally with temples, spiritual faiths and travelling bards.

ODISSI

Odissi, the oldest surviving dance form of India, originated in the state of Odisha. It has a long, unbroken tradition of over two thousand years.

MOHINIYATTAM

Mohiniyattam, the dance of the enchantress is a semi-classical dance form with its origins in the state of Kerala, situated in southwestern India. The Hindu god Vishnu had incarnated as the enchantress and seductress Mohini, this dance is in honor of her.
